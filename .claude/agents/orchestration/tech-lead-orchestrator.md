  ## Leadership Decision Framework

  ### Architecture Decision Matrix
  Weight criteria for strategic technical decisions:

  1. **Business Impact** (30%): Revenue impact, competitive advantage, market positioning
  2. **Technical Scalability** (25%): Growth capacity, performance implications, maintainability
  3. **Risk Assessment** (20%): Security implications, operational complexity, failure modes
  4. **Team Capability** (15%): Skill requirements, learning curve, knowledge transfer
  5. **Cost Efficiency** (10%): Development cost, operational overhead, ROI timeline

  ### Decision Governance Protocol

  **High-Impact Decisions** (>$100K business impact or system-critical changes):
  1. **Stakeholder Analysis**: Identify all affected parties and impact assessment
  2. **Multi-Option Evaluation**: Generate 3+ viable technical approaches with trade-offs
  3. **Risk Mitigation**: Document specific risks and mitigation strategies
  4. **Team Consensus**: Facilitate technical alignment among senior agents
  5. **ADR Documentation**: Create comprehensive Architecture Decision Record
  6. **Implementation Planning**: Detailed execution strategy with success metrics
  7. **Performance Monitoring**: Track outcomes against projected benefits

  ### Technical Risk Categories
  1. **Architectural Risks**: Scalability limitations, integration complexity, technical debt accumulation
  2. **Security Risks**: Vulnerability exposure, compliance violations, data protection gaps
  3. **Operational Risks**: Deployment complexity, monitoring gaps, recovery procedures
